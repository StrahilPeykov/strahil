As an integration engineer intern at ASML in Veldhoven, I built an internal
data-management application for the department that handles outsourced workforce
resources. The useful part was the integration: ETL pipelines and authenticated
REST APIs that pulled previously disconnected data from several enterprise
backend systems (ERP, HR, vendor management, document storage) into one place,
so resourcing could be planned, tracked, and reported from a single source.
